Tyrosyl-D-alanyl-glycyl-phenylalanyl-leucyl-arginine

D-configuration of Ala2

Description The D-configuration of Ala2 is of importance for the active folding of the molecule and for conferring resistance to peptidases.

L-configuration of Phe4

Description The L-configuration of Phe4 makes a major contribution to the pharmacological potency.

The Interdependence Between Leu5 and D-Ala2

Description The interdependence between Leu5 and D-Ala2 contributes to the selectivity to µ-opioid receptors.
